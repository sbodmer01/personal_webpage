Who I am

quick introduction.

Welcome to my little corner on the internet! I began in biochemistry, fully immersed in the world of lab experiments and research. I liked the idea of mixing compounds together in a vial, felt like I was Harry Potter in some potions class. One coding class later, I found myself equally fascinated by the creative possibilities of software, especially building tools from scratch that people actually use.

That curiosity led me to take part in some coding projects with research groups, then after my bachelors I worked in healthcare IT, and eventually came back to UC San Diego, where I'm finishing up my Master's in computer science with a specialization in AI. Whether it's apps, websites, or something in between, I'm drawn to projects that blend design, logic, and real-world usefulness.
